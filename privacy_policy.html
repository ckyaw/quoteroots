Privacy Policy

Quotidian - Daily Motivational Quotes
Effective Date: November 14, 2025
Simple Summary: Quotidian does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected.

Information We Collect

Quotidian does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device without transmitting any data to external servers.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Your favorite quotes
  • Notification preferences
  • Category selections
  • App settings

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible to you.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to deliver daily motivational quotes. These notifications:

  • Are generated entirely on your device
  • Do not require internet connectivity
  • Do not send any data to external services

Third-Party Services

Quotidian does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions completely offline and independently.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data collection practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the app or app store updates.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security concerns. All app data remains securely on your device.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app store listing or create an issue on our GitHub repository.
